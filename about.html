ABOUT US

SIL Business Services way

Our Objective

Our objective is to provide access to businesses in the emerging market to continuously tap into the vast resources of Europe for progressive development in emerging markets.

Our Mission

We understand that most emerging market businesses lack the structures and criteria to tap into the resources of Europe. Therefore we are committed to developing and providing structures that will enable emerging market businesses to overcome this challenge to tap into the resources of Europe.

Our Approach

The SIL Business Services in-house team of experts have extensive experience in businesses. This high-performance team works with our clients to invest in the basic minimum structures businesses are required to have to tap into the resource of Italy and Europe as a whole. These mostly cover documentation, websites, branding, and accounting reports. With this approach, we are able to create an acceptable bridging that helps businesses from both the emerging market and Italy to work in a value-adding manner to deliver for our clients while safely protecting our people and the environment.

BACKGROUND

Business networking is the process of establishing a mutually beneficial relationship with other business people and potential clients or customers. The primary purpose of SIL Business Services networking is to tell others about your business and expectantly turn them into customers or partners for growth.

With our incontestable expertise and exposure to businesses in emerging markets like Ghana and other advanced countries of Europe, You can depend on us to find tailor-made strategic solutions to your complex business requirements and needs. Our business philosophy is that businesses and governments in emerging markets can leapfrog via forming partnerships, acquiring world-class machinery, tools and techniques, access international standard business and engineering consultancy, as well as obtaining funding from Europe. In line with our philosophy, SIL Business Services is working with business clients across Africa to access partnership, machinery, consultancy, and funding for Italy. We strongly believe this will help businesses in less advanced countries to leapfrog and save time, create more wealth, and establish sufficient needed jobs to sustain families and social stability.

The benefits of our services include: helping you identify opportunities for partnerships, joint ventures or new areas of expansion for your business, and improving your business profile and links to the right people. In an ever-changing business climate, knowing the market is the key to developing a successful marketing plan. Sharing of knowledge and experience among peers would also allow your business to take advantage of the experiences of others before you invest time and money in a particular venture. Additionally, associating with a strong and successful business can also boost the confidence and morale of your team.

Are you thinking of taking your business to the next level?

Engage SIL Business Services to know more about accessing Italian and EU resources such as partnerships, machinery, consultancy, and funding. We offer value-adding support and advisory services to grow your business.
