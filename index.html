OUR SERVICES

We work to provide our clients with the following services:

Your link to Italian Partners

Building a successful business takes a lot of time and dedication, hence it is very prudent and significant to have network of business partners and associates to draw energy from and keep you motivated. SIL Business Services provide the partnership and networking opportunities for Italian firms in the construction, energy, infrastructure, and industrial sectors. With our wide range of services, our clients can expand their knowledge, learn from the success of others, get new clients, tell others about their business, and obtain Italian support to realize projects in Africa.

Your access to European machinery

Most machinery and tools for industry and businesses are capital equipment, meaning they require significant investment capital. Therefore getting the supplier selection and acquisition wrong could lead to financial distress or even bankruptcy issues for a company. In the area of machinery, SIL Business Services facilitate the sourcing of quality industrial machinery from Italy to Africa with funding support. We optionally go further to assist the client's trip to inspect and approve the machinery by engaging the manufacturer and to have a better understanding of the plant before placing the order.

European engineering, design, project management, and consulting to grow your business

The globalization trend has led to rising competition in all markets. Companies are seeking new ways to stand out, survive the competition, and innovate to increase revenues while keeping expenses low. Using consultants gives a refreshing insight into your business because consultants are not only committed to a single firm, but they also bring experience from variety of companies and industries. For this reason, they are highly capable of offering creative solutions and enables “out of the box” thinking. Tap into top-notch consultants from Italy to grow your business.

Access to European funding to expand your business

Funding is the fuel for companies and it is used to support working capital, acquiring tangible assets such as machinery, accessing intangible assets like license and permits, hire more staff, or even re-finance existing loans to reduce costs. Access to funding can help your company grow exponentially. With our collaboration with the Italian export credit ITARE FINANCIAL ADVISORS, we facilitate funding for capital project and purchase of industrial plant.

ABOUT US

SIL Business Services way

Our Objective

Our objective is to provide access to businesses in the emerging market to continuously tap into the vast resources of Europe for progressive development in emerging markets.

Our Mission

We understand that most emerging market businesses lack the structures and criteria to tap into the resources of Europe. Therefore we are committed to developing and providing structures that will enable emerging market businesses to overcome this challenge to tap into the resources of Europe.

Our Approach

The SIL Business Services in-house team of experts have extensive experience in businesses. This high-performance team works with our clients to invest in the basic minimum structures businesses are required to have to tap into the resource of Italy and Europe as a whole. These mostly cover documentation, websites, branding, and accounting reports. With this approach, we are able to create an acceptable bridging that helps businesses from both the emerging market and Italy to work in a value-adding manner to deliver for our clients while safely protecting our people and the environment.
